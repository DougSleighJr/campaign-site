About Leslie

Hi, I'm Leslie Sleigh, and I want to serve the families in our community by sitting on the Shepherdsville City Council. I grew up on a ranch in a small town in south Alabama, and I was always interested in politics. I wrote my Congressmen often, met two governors, and my mom would write me notes to skip school for political breakfasts.

As I got older, I grew content to let the political world take of itself without my interest. My husband and I moved to Louisville, and I happily tended to my career and family for over a decade without much interest in either national, state, or local government.

Four years ago, we moved to Shepherdsville and now again live in a community much like my hometown. I love it here! I have missed the small-town feel, and I've enjoyed getting to know people here. Our community has a need for leaders who can work well alongside others even when they disagree. I enjoy helping people reach agreements and resolving conflict when it appears. I can lead a project from start to finish and enlist effective people who want to be involved.

I once sat in a business seminar where the speaker said something I'll never forget. "Don't complain unless you have a solution to offer and are willing to be part of the change." Our city may nut run like a business, but that principle has been key for me. I no longer want to watch the politics. I want to be involved on the inside.

I am running for Shepherdsville City Council. I want to make our community stronger. If you live in Shepherdsville, I would he honored to serve you, and I greatly value your support!
